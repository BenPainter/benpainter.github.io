The Purpose

Welcome to the Hippo Zoo website! Your guide to the ever-evolving and growing video game universe created by yours truly. This website is the one-stop shop for all the news and information about the different games. You can click on the games tab to take you to a list of the games that are currently out. There you will find helpful tips for the games, summaries of the story, game trailers, and a bit of an art gallery. Or you can click on the “future and me” tab to read up about the different projects coming down the pipeline and learn a bit about me and my past. We hope you enjoy your visit and if you have any feedback on how we can improve it, don’t be shy and send us a message!
